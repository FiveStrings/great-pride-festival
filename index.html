About the Festival

Join us for the biggest celebration of the year — a full day of live music, drag performances, community, food, art, and pure joy right here in Atlantic City. All are welcome. Come as you are.

Run of Show

Here's what's happening throughout the day.

11:00 AM
🥂 VIP Tent Opens

Exclusive access for sponsors, VIP guests, and performers — till closing.

12:00 PM
🎉 Festival Kick-Off

Special appearance by Miss'd America · DJ Emme & Dillon · Pride Processional Walk · Opening Ceremony · Welcome Remarks · Sponsor Recognition

12:30 PM
🛍️ Vendor Area & Kids Zone Opens

Explore local vendors and enjoy family-friendly fun — open till closing.

1:30 – 3:00 PM
🎵 Entertainment

DJ CONST & DB Abel keep the energy going on the main stage.

3:00 – 4:15 PM
👑 Drag Showcase

Hosted by Sandy Beach & Miss Ten'e · Featuring: Hollywood J · McQueen · Fantasy · Roxy Laza · Joy Marnier · Infinity the Doll · Miss Dareena Ho

4:00 PM
🎶 Performance

Mona Lisa & Pretty Ricky Icons Tribute Band · Headliner performance by Pretty Poison featuring Jade Starling

6:00 PM
✨ Drag Finale & Closing

Drag Finale hosted by Sandy Beach · Closing Remarks

6:30 – 9:00 PM
🪩 After Party at the ByrdCage

Keep the celebration going at the ByrdCage Atlantic City!
