About the Festival

Join us for AC Pride's biggest celebration of the year — a full day of live music, drag performances, community, food, art, and pure joy right here in Atlantic City. All are welcome. Come as you are.

Run of Show

Here's what's happening throughout the day.

12:00 PM
🎉 Gates Open

Welcome to the Great Pride Festival! Pick up your wristband and explore the grounds.

1:00 PM
🎤 Opening Ceremony

Welcome remarks from AC Pride leadership and honored community guests.

2:00 PM
🎵 Live Performance

Kick off the afternoon with a live set from our featured artist — announcement coming soon!

3:30 PM
🌈 Community Showcase

Spotlighting local LGBTQIA+ artists, vendors, and community organizations.

5:00 PM
🎶 Headliner Set

Our headliner takes the main stage. You won't want to miss this.

7:00 PM
👑 Drag Extravaganza

A spectacular drag showcase featuring Atlantic City's most dazzling performers.

9:00 PM
✨ Closing Celebration

Dance the night away as we close out another unforgettable Pride Festival.
